  1. Select your preferred training program
  2. Click the "Apply for Training" button
  3. Choose your payment method (M-Pesa or PayPal)
  4. Complete the payment process
  5. You will receive training materials within 24 hours
  6. Complete the training at your own pace
  7. Submit your final assessment for certification
